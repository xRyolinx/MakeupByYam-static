MAQUILLAGE

Bridal makeup needs to tick off certain boxes: it has to last all day, complement your outfit, photograph well and look great in real life. This is where I come in with my experience, expertise and product knowledge. Using my excellent technical skills, I create looks that meet all those criteria.

My aim is to ensure that my clients look their best and their makeup is flawless. Your input and opinion are vital in achieving this. I love being creative with makeup, trying something out of the box. The looks I create for my brides are timeless, modern and chic but most importantly they reflect their personality and style.

The ultimate target is for you to feel confident and comfortable on your wedding day and that your best features are expectedly defined and enhanced.

Let me inspire you with bridal looks I created for some of my clients and take you to the world of wedding editorials where everything is possible.

FORMATION MAKEUP

Comprehensive and designed for each client individually, makeup lessons are a great way to refresh your makeup routine and learn a few new tricks. The lessons will help you to create looks you have always dreamt of.

It might be that you have just started your adventure with makeup or have been applying makeup since you were 15 – whatever your experience level is, the lesson will be tailored to your skill set and needs.

The makeup lesson will cover topics such as (but not limited to):
-How to apply the foundation
-A guide to a quick morning routine
-How to create rock star’s worthy smoky eye
-Over 40 and fabulous

But there is no limit to what we can cover, I am open to your suggestions!

The makeup lesson can take place in my home studio or in the location of your choice. There is also a virtual makeup lesson option that has been proven to work very well – please check some recent reviews here.
